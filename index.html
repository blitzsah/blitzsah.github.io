What We Do

Services & Capabilities

Ignition SCADA Development

Full-cycle development on the Inductive Automation Ignition platform — from architecture and tag design through to HMI screens, historian configuration, and deployment.

PLC & Automation Integration

Connecting SCADA systems to PLCs, RTUs, and field devices across all major protocols — Modbus, OPC-UA, EtherNet/IP, and more.

Reporting & Data Historian

Building robust data collection pipelines, Ignition Reporting module dashboards, and integration with SQL databases for operational analytics and compliance.

System Audits & Upgrades

Reviewing legacy SCADA systems, identifying risks and performance gaps, then planning and executing modernisation — including migrations to Ignition.

Training & Knowledge Transfer

Upskilling your internal teams on Ignition platform usage, best practices, and day-to-day system management — so you stay in control after delivery.

Contract & Project Support

Available for short or long-term contracts. Whether embedded with your team on-site or working remotely, Harts Consulting fits around your project needs.
